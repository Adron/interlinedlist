# Organizations

Organizations let you belong to groups and collaborate with others.

## Joining Organizations

- All users automatically join **"The Public"** — a system organization
- Browse **Organizations** from the top nav to see public organizations
- Click **Join** to become a member of a public organization

## Creating an Organization

1. Go to **Organizations**
2. Click **New Organization**
3. Enter a name, description, and set visibility (public or private)
4. You'll be the owner and can add members

## Roles

- **Owner**: Full control; can delete the org and manage all members
- **Admin**: Can add/remove members and change roles (except owner)
- **Member**: Basic access

## Public vs Private

- **Public**: Anyone can see and join
- **Private**: Invite-only; members must be added by an owner or admin

## Managing Members

As an owner or admin:

1. Open your organization
2. Go to the Members section
3. Add users by ID or invite, remove members, or change roles

## Your Organizations

Go to **User** → **Organizations** in the dropdown to see all organizations you belong to and your role in each.
