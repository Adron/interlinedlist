# Getting Started

Welcome to InterlinedList! This guide will help you get oriented with the platform.

## Dashboard

The **Dashboard** is your home base. From the top navigation, click the chart icon to go to your dashboard. Here you'll see:

- A feed of messages from you and people you follow
- Message input to post new updates
- Quick access to your lists and organizations

## Navigation

Use the top bar to move around:

- **Home** — Landing page
- **Dashboard** — Your message feed
- **Lists** — Create and manage your lists
- **Organizations** — Browse and join organizations

Click your profile icon in the top right for the dropdown menu:

- **Settings** — Profile, preferences, security
- **Help** — This documentation
- **Logout** — Sign out

## First Steps

1. **Post a message** — Use the input on the dashboard to share an update
2. **Create a list** — Go to Lists and click "New List" to add your first list
3. **Update your profile** — Visit Settings to add a display name, avatar, and bio

## Need More Help?

Use the sidebar to explore other topics. See **Lists** for creating and managing lists, **Messages** for posting and visibility, and **Settings** for customizing your experience.
