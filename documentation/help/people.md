# People

The People page helps you manage followers, following, and follow requests.

## Access

Go to **People** from the top navigation, or visit your profile and click **Followers** or **Following**.

## Features

- **Follower count**: See how many people follow you
- **Following count**: See how many people you follow
- **View followers**: Click to see the list of people who follow you
- **View following**: Click to see the list of people you follow
- **Follow / Unfollow**: From any user profile, click to follow or unfollow

## Pending Follow Requests

If your account is **private** (Settings â†’ Permissions), new followers must request to follow you. The People page shows pending requests. You can approve or reject each request. Approved users become followers and can see your public messages.

## Private Accounts

When you enable **Private Account** in Settings:

- New followers must request to follow you
- You approve or reject each request
- Existing followers remain unchanged
- Your viewing preference (e.g., Following Only) affects what messages you see from people you follow
