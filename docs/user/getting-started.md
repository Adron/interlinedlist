# Getting Started with InterlinedList

Welcome to InterlinedList! This guide will help you get started using the platform.

## What is InterlinedList?

InterlinedList is a time-series based micro-blogging platform similar to Mastodon, with embedded DSL scripts for creating interactive lists. Share your thoughts, create interactive lists, and connect with others in a decentralized social space.

## Creating an Account

### Registration

1. Navigate to the registration page (`/register`)
2. Fill in the required information:
   - **Username**: 3-30 characters, alphanumeric and underscores only, must start with a letter
   - **Email**: A valid email address
   - **Password**: Must meet the following requirements:
     - At least 8 characters long
     - Contains at least one uppercase letter
     - Contains at least one lowercase letter
     - Contains at least one number
     - Contains at least one special character
3. Click "Register" to create your account

### Email Verification

After registration, you'll receive an email verification link. Click the link to verify your email address and activate your account.

**Note**: In development mode, the verification token may be displayed in the registration response for testing purposes.

## Logging In

1. Navigate to the login page
2. Enter your email or username
3. Enter your password
4. Click "Login"

You'll be automatically logged in and redirected to your dashboard.

## Your Profile

### Viewing Your Profile

Access your profile from the navigation menu to view:
- Your username and display name
- Your bio
- Your avatar
- Account creation date
- Email verification status

### Updating Your Profile

You can update the following information:
- Display name
- Bio
- Avatar URL

Changes are saved automatically when you submit the form.

## Password Management

### Changing Your Password

1. Go to your account settings
2. Select "Change Password"
3. Enter your current password
4. Enter your new password (must meet strength requirements)
5. Confirm your new password
6. Click "Update Password"

### Resetting a Forgotten Password

1. Click "Forgot Password" on the login page
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your email for a password reset link
4. Click the link and follow the instructions to set a new password

**Note**: Password reset tokens expire after a set period for security.

## Security Best Practices

- Use a strong, unique password
- Enable two-factor authentication when available
- Keep your email address up to date
- Log out from shared devices
- Report suspicious activity immediately
